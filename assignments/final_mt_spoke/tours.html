Daily tours

Currently there are three tours that you may do on your own or with one of our tour guides. There are three different skills levels, and fun on every trail.

Gallagator Trail

This trail is a great choice for families, as it takes you off road through town. This tour passes by duck ponds, and ends at the Museum of the Rockies.

  • Location: Bozeman, MT - runs SW from South Church to the Museum of the Rockies on Kagy Boulevard.
  • Skill Level: easy off road trail in town.
  • Distance: 2.2 total miles.
  • Cost: $20/cyclist( family/group rates may apply, call for details)

West Bridger/Foothills Road

This trail offers beautiful scenery along the wester flank of Bridger range.

  • Location: North Edge of Bozeman Montana, along the western flank of the Bridger range.
  • Type: Mountain bike trail.
  • Distance: 13.7 total miles
  • Skill Level: Easy to Moderate
  • Cost: $125/cyclist (group rates may apply, call for details)

Emerald Lake/Heather Lake

This mountain bike tour offers beautiful scenery as well an exciting adventure

  • Location: South of Bozeman down Hyalite Canyon Road about one mile past the waterfall.
  • Type: 100% single track mountain bike trail
  • Distance: 9 total miles.
  • Skill Level: Advanced.
  • Cost: $95/cyclist (group rates may apply. call for details).
