About Us

Here at The Mountain Spoke, we specialize in bicycles from repairs and parts to tours and events. We are a small retail shop located in Bozeman, MT, and we cater to bicycle enthusiasts from all around the region. In addition to sales and repairs, we also organize and sponsor tours from serious bicyclists from around the world. Stop by our shop or website to check out all that we have to offer.
