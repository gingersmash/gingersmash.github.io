Cycle The Continental Divide

This race takes place on a remote trail spanning along the Great Continental Divide from, Banff, Canada, to Antelope Wells on the US/Mexico border. This is a total of more than 2,768 miles. The entire route is dirt-road and mountain pass riding. The total elevation gain is over 200,000 feet. About 80% of it is composed of county, Forest Service, and Bureau of Land Management dirt and gravel roads. The remaining portion is made up of 10% four-wheel-drive tracks and single track trails, and 10% paved roads. This year celebrates the 20th anniversary of the Great Divide Mountain Bike Route. (GDMBR)If you are looking for an unforgettable adventure, sign up now for the ride of your life.

Race Details

  • When: June 8, 2018
  • Time: 8:00 AM
  • Cost: FREE
  • Where: All participants will meet at the Grand Depart in Banff, Alberta, Canada
